Argentina Fuel Price Intelligence Dashboard
Executive-Level Business Intelligence Portfolio Project
Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Fuel Price Intelligence Dashboard for Argentina built using modern Business Intelligence tools including Power BI, Tableau, and Looker Studio.

The objective of the project is to transform raw fuel market data into executive-ready business insights, helping stakeholders understand pricing trends, regional variations, and revenue patterns across the national fuel market.

Professional analytics platforms such as Tableau and Looker are widely used by organizations to convert data into strategic decisions, and this project demonstrates those real-world skills.

Business Problem

Fuel prices in Argentina vary significantly across regions, fuel types, and companies. Without a centralized dashboard, decision-makers cannot easily:

Identify long-term price trends
Compare price variations across provinces
Understand which fuel types generate the highest revenue
Track top companies by revenue
Analyze price volatility across years

This dashboard solves that problem by converting complex datasets into clear business intelligence insights.

Key Insights Delivered
Market Trend Insights
Strong price growth trend from 2021 to 2023
Long-term price movement analyzed across multiple years
Clear identification of price volatility across fuel types
Revenue & Product Insights
Identification of top-performing fuel products
Revenue contribution by product type
Total market revenue analysis
Geographic Insights
Province-level price comparison
Regional price distribution patterns
Identification of high-price vs low-price regions
Business Insights
Top 10 companies by revenue
Price comparison between companies
Market growth insights

Projects created using the official fuel-price dataset also focus on price trends, regional comparisons, and company performance — which this project successfully demonstrates.
